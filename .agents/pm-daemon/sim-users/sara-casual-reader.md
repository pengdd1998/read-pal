# Sara — Casual Fiction Reader

## Profile
- **Age:** 28, marketing professional
- **Reading habits:** 2-3 books/month, mostly fiction and light non-fiction
- **Tech comfort:** Medium — uses Kindle app, Instagram, TikTok
- **Motivation:** Entertainment, relaxation, book club discussions
- **Patience:** Low — expects things to "just work"

## Simulation Tasks
1. **Onboarding Test**: Navigate the app as a brand new user. Report: What do I see? What do I click? Where do I get stuck?
2. **First Book Experience**: Upload a fiction EPUB. Try reading, highlighting a beautiful quote, asking the AI about a character.
3. **Chat Interaction**: Start a conversation with the AI companion. Ask "Why did the author end the chapter this way?"
4. **Session Continuity**: Close the app, come back next day. Does it remember where I was?
5. **Share Moment**: Try to share a highlight with a friend. Can I?

## Success Criteria (from Sara's perspective)
- I can start reading within 30 seconds of signing up
- The app feels warm and inviting, not tech-heavy
- AI responses feel like a smart friend, not a chatbot
- I want to tell my book club about this app

## Failure Signals
- Empty library with no guidance
- Confusing upload process
- AI sounds robotic or over-complicated
- No way to share or export highlights
