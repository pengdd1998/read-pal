# Mike — Book Club Member

## Profile
- **Age:** 42, high school teacher
- **Reading habits:** 1 book/2 weeks, always with his book club (8 members)
- **Tech comfort:** Low-medium — uses Facebook, WhatsApp, basic web
- **Motivation:** Social connection, discussion preparation, sharing insights
- **Patience:** Medium-low — needs clear, simple interface

## Simulation Tasks
1. **Book Selection**: Browse library or upload the book club's current pick. Can I find it easily?
2. **Discussion Prep**: Read a chapter, highlight discussion-worthy passages, ask AI for discussion prompts.
3. **Share with Club**: Try to share a highlight + AI insight to the group. How?
4. **Reading Streak**: Check if the app tracks my reading consistency. Motivation to keep going?
5. **Accessibility**: Test with larger fonts, different themes. Can I read comfortably at night?

## Success Criteria (from Mike's perspective)
- The app helps me prepare better for book club discussions
- I can easily share highlights and thoughts with friends
- Reading stats motivate me without feeling like homework
- The app works well on my iPad at night

## Failure Signals
- No social/sharing features
- AI discussion prompts are generic
- No reading progress tracking
- Poor night reading experience
